Felix Ettensperger, M.A.

Hi, my name is Felix Ettensperger, I'm 32 years old and living in Germany. I'm currently a Ph.D. student at the University of Freiburg.

I'm working in quantitative conflict research developing computer models, simulations and algorithms to predict inner-state conflicts worldwide.

In the last year I have been working extensively with Machine Learning Algorithms (Random Forest, k-NN, Hard-Competitive Clustering and Neural Gas) and with Artificial Neural Networks using Keras, Theano and TensorFlow.
I'm further interested in text mining, quantitative content analysis and artificial text generation.

About 3 years ago I started working intensively with R and about 1 year ago I started with Python.

I'm currently also the Assistant Managing Editor of the academic peer-reviewed Journal Statistics, Politics and Policy.
